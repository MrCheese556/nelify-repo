Number of cars: 250

Explanation:

This is a demonstration of a neural network. You can train your own car to drive, and then try to race against it. (You will probably lose.)

To start, just hit the refresh button in the center.

Each time you do this, a new generation of cars are spawned, and the amount is specified on the left.

Only choose a high amount (Above 500) if your computer can handle it. When you are running a simulation and see a car pull ahead (Wait a few seconds first), click the save button.

The algorithm works similar to evolution, where each new generation of cars has the same brain as the saved one (The best model), but with a little mutation.

The more times you save, reload, and mutate the cars, the better the entire species will become.

If you ever want to race with your cars, click the red car button, which will spawn a drivable (Arrow keys) car.

Good luck (Hover on this message to see the parts of the network)
